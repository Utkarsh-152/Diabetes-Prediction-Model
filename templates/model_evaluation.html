Model Interpretation

Our XGBoost model demonstrates strong performance with an accuracy of 86.5% and a precision of 93.8%. This means that when our model predicts a patient has diabetes, it is correct 93.8% of the time, which is crucial for minimizing false alarms.

The recall of 78.3% indicates that our model correctly identifies about 78% of all diabetes cases. While this is good, it also means that approximately 22% of diabetes cases might be missed. We're continuously working to improve this metric to ensure fewer cases go undetected.

The F1 score of 85.4% represents a good balance between precision and recall, indicating that our model performs well overall in diabetes prediction tasks.
